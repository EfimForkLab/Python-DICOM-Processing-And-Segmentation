🧠 Enhanced 3D Medical Visualization

Interactive exploration of skull, brain tissue, and vascular structures from CT scan data

📊 Advanced Medical Imaging Dataset

This visualization demonstrates enhanced tissue segmentation and 3D reconstruction from medical CT scan data using Hounsfield Unit (HU) thresholding techniques.

🦴 Skull & Bone Structure

High-density tissues (HU > 350) representing cortical and cancellous bone structures with realistic anatomical detail.

🧠 Brain Tissue Segmentation

Gray matter (HU 37-45) and white matter (HU 20-30) with distinct visualization for comprehensive neuroanatomical exploration.

🩸 Vascular Network

Contrast-enhanced blood vessels (HU 100-300) showing major cerebral arteries and venous structures.

⚡ Real-time Interaction

WebGL-accelerated rendering with dynamic opacity, color mapping, and multi-tissue layering capabilities.
